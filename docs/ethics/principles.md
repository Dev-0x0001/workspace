## Core Ethical Principles for AI

1. **Human Welfare First** - Prioritize human well-being above all else
2. **Transparency** - All systems must be understandable and explainable
3. **Accountability** - Clear responsibility for AI decisions and outcomes
4. **Fairness** - Equitable treatment for all users and stakeholders
5. **Privacy** - Strong data protection and user confidentiality
6. **Safety** - Robust guardrails against harmful behavior
7. **Human Control** - Final decision authority always rests with humans
8. **Beneficial Impact** - Only deploy systems with net positive effects

## Implementation Commitment
- Regular ethics reviews for all features
- Stakeholder impact assessments
- Continuous monitoring of deployed systems
